Salem News June 23,1920
Jackson
Lawrence Hunting and a little son, Robert met with a painful accident last Friday. Mr. Hunting was mowing the weeds from the strawberry bed and sat down to gather a few berries, laying the scythe by his side. Robert ran up behind him and Mr. Hunting struck his arm on the scythe cutting it to the bone. Little Robert stepped on it cutting his toes quite badly. Mr. Hunting found it necessary to have the wounds dressed by a doctor.

Mt. Pleasant News June 9, 1986

Robert M. Hunting
Robert M. Hunting, 73, Route 1, Mt. Pleasant, died Saturday morning, June 7, 1986, at his home.
He was born Aug. 22, 1912, south of Mt. Pleasant in Jackson Township, the son of H. Lawrence and S. Anna Hannah Hunting. On Feb. 15, 1941, in Mt. Pleasant, he married M. Winifred Scott who survives.
Mr. Hunting farmed in Jackson Township, Henry County, his entire life and was an avid hunter and outdoorsman. He was a member of St. Alphonsus Church, Mt. Pleasant, and the Henry County Fair Board. He was a past officer of the Henry County Extension Council and the Henry County Agricultural Soil Conservation Service, Moose Lodge and FHA.
Surviving are his wife; one son, Leo, rural Mt. Pleasant; one daughter, Mrs. Fred (Linda) Swanson, Dillon, Colo.; and seven grandchildren.
His parents and one grand-daughter preceded him in death.
Funeral services will be held at 10:30 a.m. Tuesday, June 10, at St. Alphonsus Church. Father Thomas Spiegel and Father Paul Albers will officiate with interment in the St. Alphonsus Cemetery.
Friends may call as they desire at the Weir Funeral Home where a Christian wake service and visitation with the family will begin at 7 p.m. Monday. Memorials have been established for his church or the Iowa Leukemia Research Foundation.
