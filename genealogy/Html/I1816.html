Salem News August 31, 1916
COX- DONALDSON
Last Sunday at 8:00 a.m. occurred the marriage, at the home of the bride southwest of town, of Mr. Walter Cox and Miss Ada Donaldson, Rev. R.W. Luce performing the ceremony.
It was a quiet wedding, none but the immediate relatives being present. After a wedding breakfast the happy couple departed in their Ford on their wedding trip to the state fair at Des Moines.
The groom is a son of Mr. and Mrs. Barclay Cox and is an industrious and frugal young man. The bride is the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. S.F. Donaldson, a most worthy young lady. Both are held in high esteem by their many friends. They will reside with the groom's parents on the old home place. They have the best wishes of all.
