From his M ?I? to discharge he held the rank of Wagoner Private and the rolls show him present except as follows: August 8-August 31 1863 left Sick at Regimental Hospital; October 31, 1863 left sick at Camp Magnolia since August 29, 1863.

Reinlisted as a Veteran Volunteer January 1, 1864.

St Joseph News Press, St Joseph Missouri August 21, 1919

Alberts S Stark, seventy-two years old, veteran of the Civil War, died at 9:15 o'clock last night at his home, 802 Main Street of injuries sustained July 6 when he fell down a flight of stairs at his home, fracturing his hip. He was a widower, and is survived by two sons Charles Stark, San Francisco and Frank Davis, Fort Morgan, Colo and two daughters, Mrs Dora Bowman of Falls City, Neb., and Mrs Carrie Knapp of St Joseph. A grandson, Charles Reaves of St Joseph also survives him.
