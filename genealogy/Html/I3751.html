Alice Barr

Alice N. Barr, 98, of Sunrise Terrace Care Center in Winfield, died Saturday evening, March 9, 2002, at the care center.

Born Oct. 15, 1903, in Henry County, she was the daughter of Macey and Minnie Hummell Hockett. On April 20, 1939, she married Nels Barr. Hedied Jan. 11, 1956.

Mrs. Barr enjoyed canning, sewing, playing the organ, quilting and making rugs. She also loved the farm and the work involved.

Survivors include one daughter, Orvilla Barr of Sunrise Terrace Care Center; one niece; seven nephews; three stepgrandchildren; nine stepgreatgrandchildren; and numerous stepgreatgreatgrandchildren.

Besides her husband, she was preceded in death by her parents, one infant son and several sisters.

Open visitation will be from 2 p.m. today until the time of service Wednesday at Beatty Funeral Home in Mount Pleasant.

The funeral for Mrs. Barr will be at 2 p.m. Wednesday at Beatty Funeral Home, with Pastor Tom Palmer of Salem Friends Church officiating. Burial will be in Forrest Home Cemetery in Mount Pleasant.
