Mt. Pleasant News, Feb. 15, 1943
Marion Harshbarger
Taken By Death
Salem - Marion Harshbarger, 80, passed away at his home Sunday evening about 5:30. The body was brought to the Stevens Funeral Home in Salem.
Funeral services will be held Tuesday afternoon at 2 o'clock form the Salem Methodist church with the Rev. B. G. Hankins officiating. Burial will be in the new addition to the East cemetery.
Mr. Harshbarger is survived by his wife, Mrs. Etta Harshbarger, and two daughters, Mrs. Ray Trueblood near Salem, and Miss Viola Harshbarger, who teaches in the schools at Wyman.
