Mt. Pleasant Journal, November 12, 1874

SAD ACCIDENT AND DEATH. - Wm. Seaton, brother of B. F. Seaton, of Boyleston, was engaged on Monday last in digging a cistern, when it caved in upon him, completely covering him with a heavy mass of earth. A neighbor who was assisting him immediately gave the alarm and sprangin to assist him. He was still gasping when reached, but immediately expired. All efforts to restore him were vain. He leaves several motherless children to mourn their double bereavement.

Tombstone info., died aged 49 yrs, 4 mos., 7 days
