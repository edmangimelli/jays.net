From Wenatchee World- on line 4-2-2000
Martha Elizabeth Voss

Martha Elizabeth Voss, 82, of East Wenatchee, died Friday, March 31, 2000, at Central Washington Hospital.

She had lived in East Wenatchee for two years and was a retired waitress.

Survivors include a son, Duane Wilson of Wenatchee; and two sisters, Leona Sharpe of Beaverton, Ore., and Mabel Byers.

Private services will be held at Eastlawn Cemetery in Beloit, Wis. Local arrangements are by Telford's Chapel of the Valley, East Wenatchee.
