I taught country school . Was a housewife after my husbands death. Then worked as an aide in Memorial Hospital in Mt. Pleasant, Ia for seven years. I retired and moved to Monrovia , Calif.

Salem News March 18, 1926
WHITE'S INSTITUTE- Bertha McDowell gave a birthday party Sat. afternoon for her daughter Ruby. Those enjoying the afternoon and birthday lunch were Wilma and Helen Johnson, Mildred, Francis and Maxine Douglass and Alice Peterson.

Salem News May 4, 1916
JACKSON TWP.- Mr. Harry McDowell and Miss Bertha Hannah of MorningSun were united in Marriage Sunday at high noon at the home of her parents, Mr. and Mrs. J.W. Hannah of Morning Sun. The young couple have many friends who wish them happiness.

Salem News April 29, 1926
White's Institute- Mr. and Mrs. Hannah and daughter of Morning Sun visited their daughter and sister Mrs. Harry McDowell last Sunday.- Mr. and Mrs. Harry McDowell celebrated their 10th wedding anniversary Friday the 23d by entertaining for supper Clarence Peterson and family, Walter Tatum and family and Ruth Husband.
