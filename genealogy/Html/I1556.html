The Salem Weekly News March 18,1909
LUCINDA MACY
----
Lucinda Macy was born in Wayne county, Indiana, Oct. 21, 1830; came to Iowa in 1855 and settled near Salem, where she resided during her life in Iowa.
Over fifty ears ago she gave her heart to God, and has been a faithful and consistent member during that time, the M.E. church being the church of her choice.
She died at the home of her sister in Salem, Mrs. Benj. Long, March 15, 1909, aged 79 years, 5 mo., 13 days. She is survived by two sistersand three brothers.
Funeral services were held at the M.E. church Tuesday at 2 p.m., conducted by the pastor, Rev. G. W. Barber. Interment in M.E. cemetery.
