ANNA A. JOY
PLAINVILLE - A former Zurich resident, Anna A. Joy, 82, died Wednesday, April 29, at the Rooks County Home.
She was born Dec. 5, 1898 at Hays, the daughter of Franklin M. and Anna M. (Weber) Joy. Her husband James L. Joy, died in 1968.
She was a member of the St. Andrew Episcopal Church in rural Hays.
Survivors include three daughters, Thelma Eichman and Norma Gagnon, both of Zurich, and Frances Eichman, Palco; one brother, Milton Joy, Hays; two sisters, Eartha Pugh, WaKeeney, and Stephana Barns, WaKeeney; nine grandchildren and 10 great grandchildren.
Funeral services will be at 10 a.m. Friday at St. Michael Episcopal Church, Hays, the Revs. Robert Ehlifeld, F. W. Litchman, and David T. Agnew officiating; burial in Mt. Allen Cemetery, Hays. Friends may call until the hour of service at Moshel's Funeral Home, Plainville. Memorials may be made to the St. Andrew Episcopal Church.
