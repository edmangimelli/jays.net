The Salem Weekly News May 8, 1941
RALPH MCDOWELL
Ralph , infant son of Ernest and Grace McDowell, was born April 28, and died on April 30, 1941 at the home of his parents, northwest of Salem.
The little one did not linger long with loved ones, but shall be missed by the parents and sister and two brothers, Opal and Hazel, Marlo and Arlo.
Interment was made in East cemetery, where Mrs. Bessie F. Collins read the Scripture lesson from Matt. 18: 10-14 and a few remarks based on2 Sam. 12: 23 followed with prayer.
"As the sweet flower that scents the morn.
But withers in the rising day.
Thus lovely was this infant's dawn,
Thus swiftly find its life away."
