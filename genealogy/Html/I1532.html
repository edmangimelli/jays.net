Notes:
Free Press(Mt. Pleasant, Ia.) May 6, 1915
Salem
Mrs. Benjamin Long died Sunday morning, April 25th. The funeral was held in the M. E. church of Salem Monday at 2:00 o'clock. Services conducted by Rev. Hehner assisted by Rev. Miss Smith of the Friend's church. Mrs. Long was 82 years old and leaves one son John, of Salem and three daughters, besides grandchildren and great grandchildren and numerous friends and acquaintances. Interment was made in the Salem cemetery.
