Notes:
Salem News 2-23 - 1905
The Wallbank farm, now occupied by Wm. Gay, has been purchased by Clarence Bunker. There are 202 acres , and the consideration was $8,000. Mr. Gay and family expect to move into their Salem propertyin the near future.

Salem News November 9, 1916
Clarence Bunker of Stockport has purchased the Masden farm southeast of Salem from Dr. W.L. Stewart. It consists of 120 acres. Consideration, $16,000.

Salem News January 18, 1917
Clarence Bunker and family moved over from Stockport, Tuesday ontothe John Masden farm recently purchased by Mr. Bunker. Their many Salem friends welcome the return of this family to this community.
