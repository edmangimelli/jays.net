Notes:
Salem News Oct. 26, 1922
CELEBRATED EIGHTY - EIGHTH BIRTHDAY
Last Thursday Mr. and Mrs. Harlan Pickard's home was the scene of a happy and social gathering in honor of Grandma Hannah who lives with them, it being her 88th birthday.
Four generations were present - her four children, twelve grandchildren, twelve great- grandchildren; two grandchildren and five great - grandchildren were unable to be present.
Those present were , Miss Katharine at home, Mr. and Mrs. Frank Hannah, Mr. and Mrs. Raymond Hannah, Mrs. J. R. Hannah , Marcia and Bennie; Mr .and Mrs. Robert Hannah, Mr .and Mrs. Leo Hannah and son Marlo, Mr. and Mrs. Lawrence Hunting, Mr .and Mrs. Harry McDowell and family, Mrs. Sarah McDowell, Mr. and Mrs. Owen Pickard and family, Mr. and Mrs. Dewey Pickard and baby Marguerite of Morning Sun, Mr. J. W. Hannah, Misses Belle and Esther; Mrs. Sam Walker and baby Viola, Mrs. Matt. Hickenbottom and son James. Mr. and Mrs. Leslie Pickard, bothof whom teach school at Stockport, came over after school and spent the evening.
The fine dinner was served cafeteria style as each had come bringing well- filled baskets.
Everyone enjoyed the day, departing wishing Grandma many more happy birthdays.
