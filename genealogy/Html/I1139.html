Salem News February 17, 1916
JACKSON TWP.- Feb. 15,.- Philip Franzman of Francis, Nebraska, visited at the home of his brother- in - law, A.L. Byers the week end. Mr. and Mrs. Byers and little Verle, Mr. Franzman, Harvey Byers, wife and little daughter were Sunday guests at the George Bunker home. A very pleasant day was enjoyed by all as it has been some time since Mr. Franzman visited here. Mr. Bunker's friends will be glad to learn he is much improved from his recent illness.
