Hays Daily News Dec. 28, 1948
John McIntosh, 73
Dies At Home Here
John Alexander McIntosh, 73, died late yesterday afternoon at his home on East 17 th street after an illness of four months.
Mr. McIntosh was born February 24, 1875 in Green County, Ia., and came with his parents, Cordelia and James McIntosh to Ellis county two years later. On January 26, 1898 he was married to Rose Helena Haddock and they had ten children, one of whom died as an infant.
They are surviving, the widow an these children; Mrs. John Simonson of Olympia, Wash., James W. McIntosh, Oregon City Ore.; Fred McIntosh, Oregon City, Ore.; Mrs. O. L. Hunley, San Diego, Calif.; Lawrence McIntosh, Collyer; Frank McIntosh; Mrs. A. F. Debes; Mrs. Tom Payne and Mrs. Wayne Cook of Hays. There is one brother, George of Hays and a sister Mrs. Clara Balls of Hays. There are 83 grandchildren and three great grandchildren.
Funeral services will be at 10:30 o'clock Thursday morning at the Morford Mortuary.
