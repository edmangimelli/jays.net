George Ollie Oakley

Birth: 18 JUL 1894, Kings Lynn, England
Death: 11 DEC 1970, Morning Sun Ia.

Married Cora Belle Hannah on 2 JUL 1940 at Morning Sun, Ia.

m.Cora Belle Hannah

Notes:
Ollie came to the United States with his father in March of 1913. He bacame a U.S. citizen and served in WW I. Was a member of Presbyterian Church and of Barracks 2327 Veterans of WWI. Belle and Ollie liver 2 miles west of Morning Sun, Ia. on a farm. Ollie died of Diabetes and of heart trouble. Belle died of cancer of the stomach.
