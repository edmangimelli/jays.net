Salem News 7-20-1922
Spray- Donaldson
The marriage of Miss Ruth Spray and Mr. Forrest Donaldson occurred Wednesday, July 19 at the home of Rev. J.W .Stine, Burlington. Following the ceremony a wedding supper was served at the home of the grooms sister in Burlington, Mrs. Jess Kerr. The bride is the daughter of Mrs. Margaret Spray, the groom being the son of Mr. and Mrs. S.F. Donaldson. Both are most excellent young people and have hosts of friends. They will reside on their farm near Hillsboro. The news joins their friends in extending congratulations.
Mrs. Roy Smith and children are visiting her parents, Mr. and Mrs. Fred Taylor. Mr. Smith is working in a barber shop in Rock Island, Ill.
