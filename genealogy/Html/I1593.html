Salem Weekly News April 10, 1930
ROBERT B. ARMER
Robert Bruce Armer, oldest son of James and Maria Armer was born in Pennsylvania December 7, 1857. he came with his parents to Iowa at about 7 years of age. He was married to Mary F. Hunting Sept. 2, 1886 who preceded him in death May 1, 1922. To this union was born one child, Margaret.
He had lived for about forty years in the Donaldson chapel community. His health began to fail him about four years ago. He fought courageously to hold off the hand of death. He was cared for duringthe last few months of his illness by his nephew and niece, Mr. and Mrs. Walter Elmore.
He is survived by his daughter, Margaret Baldosier; one grandson, four sisters - Mrs. Ellen Long, Mrs. Mary Goldsmith, Mrs. Laura McFay of Salem and Mrs. Etta Harshbarger of Mt. Pleasant, besides hosts of relativeand friends.
Funeral services were held Monday morning at Donaldson chapel, conducted by the Rev. R. L. Brown, Interment in the east cemetery at Salem.
