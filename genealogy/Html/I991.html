Mary C. "Barbara" Hannah

Notes:
Mt. Pleasant Journal March 9, 1882
DIED
In Jackson township, Feb. 12, 1882, of paralysis of the brain, Mary, daughter of John and Katie Hannah.
Mary was a young lady of quiet deportment and studious habits and by her genial disposition had gained the esteem of all who knew her andshe will be sadly missed in the home and the social circle of this community. The funeral discourse delivered by Rev. J. S. Ferguson, at Donaldson chapel was attended by a large concourse of friends. Thus passed away in the morning of life one who gave promise of much usefulness. The grief stricken parents have the sympathy of the entire community in this hour of trial.
