Notes:
From the Deed records of Henry Co Kentucky (R Wysong research)
1. 14 Sep or 14 Feb 1845 (think Feb is correct, see the next transaction)
William Stark, one of the heirs of Reuben Stark, of Parke Co IN sells to
Preston Stark for $400 land on the waters of Floyd Fork 53+ acres and being
Lot no. 4 in the division of the lands of Reuben Stark.

2. Given that Preston Stark has sold the 53 acres William sold him to
Jesse G. Wilhoit of Oldham County, Rebecca Stark releases her rights to the
property for $1. 3 Mar 1845.

3. 18 Oct 1845 William sells to Jesse T. Wilhoit for $152.50 all his undivided interest in and to the dower of his deceased mother, Sarah Stark, hers as the widow of Reuben Stark, about 15 acres
