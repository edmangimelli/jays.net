Death of Henry H Stark
Henry H Stark, who was well known in Fairfield and Jefferson county, died at his home in this city Monday morning at 2 o'clock, after a protracted illness. He was born in Park Co., Ind., February 6th 1839, where he grew to manhood. He was married in that state April 22nd, 1860, to Miss Sarah E. Sappenfield, who survives him. A short time later the young people removed to Jefferson county, and the home of the family has since been here. Two children were born of this marriage, one of whom, Hiram Stark of Fairfield township, is yet living. Mr Stark was an affectionate husband and father, and many friends will mourn his death and extend their sympathies to the bereaved wife. Funeral services will be held at the family home this afternoon at 2 o'clock, and will be conducted by W.C.Shepherd. The body will be interred in the city cemetery.--Fairfield Ledger 1-26-1898

Death of H H Stark
After an illness lasting over six months Mr Henry H Stark passed peacefully away Monday morning at half past three o'clock. Deceased was fifty-eight years, eleven months and eighteen days old.
Mr Stark had lived in this county for over thirty-seven years, coming here in 1860 from Park county, Indiana, where he was born February 6, 1839. He was a son of Mr and Mrs Wm. A Stark and one of a large family of children. He was married in Park county, Indiana on the twenty-sixth of April, 1860 to Sarah E Sappenfield, and in the fall of that year came to this county. They lived on a farm five miles west of town...
