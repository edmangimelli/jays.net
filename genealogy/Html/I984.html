Notes:
The Hawk-Eye
June 11, 1989
Robert Jay Long
Robert Jay Long, 57, of Salem, died at 9:10 p.m. Friday, June 9, 1989, at the veteran's Administration Medical Center, Iowa City.
Born March 25, 1932, in Salem, he was the son of Grover and Luella Miller Long.
He was a carpenter.
Surviving are one brother, Harold Long, Covington, La.; three sisters, Mrs. Dorothy Watson, Cambridge, Idaho., Mrs. Liberta Taylor, Roseville, Ill., Mrs. Jean Brombaugh, Stockport.
Visitation will be after noon Sunday with the family receiving friends from 2 to 4 p.m. Sunday at the Beatty-Wilkins Funeral Home, Mount Pleasant.
The service for Mr. Long will be at 2 p.m. Monday at the Salem Friends Church, with the Rev. Dwight Engel officiating. Burial will be in the Salem East Cemetery.
A memorial has been established for he Salem East Cemetery.
