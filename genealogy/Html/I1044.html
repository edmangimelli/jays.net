West Burlington girl wins three Ellsworth scholarships

The Hawk Eye

Sulene Nelson, daughter of Steven and Patricia Nelson of West Burlington, has been awarded an Ellsworth Leadership Scholarship ($400), an Ellsworth Merit Scholarship ($400) and an Ellsworth Business Scholarship ($400) for the 2001-2002 school year. She is a senior at West Burlington High School and will major in accounting. Ellsworth is a comprehensive community college located in Iowa Falls.

BHS student captures national soccer honor

Sarah Cox, daughter of Lori and Darryl Cox of Burlington, has been named a United States National Award winner in soccer by the United States Achievement Academy.

Cox, who attends Burlington High School, was nominated for this national award by Susan Cohoon, a teacher and soccer coach at the school. Shewill appear in the United States Achievement Academy Official yearbook, which is published nationally.

Cox is the granddaughter of Mr. and Mrs. Ralph Reeves and Mr. and Mrs. Maston Cox, all of Burlington.
