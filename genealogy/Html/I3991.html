The Salem Weekly News, August 1, 1935
EDWIN CHAPMAN
-----
Edwin Chapman, an aged resident of Glasgow vicinity died Wednesday night of last week at the hoem of his nephew, Bert Humphrey, with whom he has made his home for some years. Mr. Chapman was the son of Georgeand Suzanne (Endersby) Chapman and was born June 14, 1853 in Glasgow, Jefferson county. He was 83 years, 1 mon. and 10 days of age. He is survived by one brother, W. C. Chapman of Topeka, Kas. Several brothers and sisters and his parents had preceded him in death.
Funeral services were held in Glasgow M. E. church, Friday afternoon and the remains were buried in the Glasgow cemetery.
