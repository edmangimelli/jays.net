MAXINE SAUBERMAN & DAVID RICCI
Maxine Sharon Sauberman and David Louis Ricci were married April 15 at the Hyatt Regency Hotel in Burlingame with Rabbi David Roller officiating.
The bride is the daughter of Rhoda and David Sauberman of Martinez. She is the sister of Mitchell Sauberman of Concord. Grandparents are Pauline Markowitz of Walnut Creek, the late Aaron Markowitz, the late Louis Sauberman and the late Ruth Sauberman.
The groom is the son of Jan and Louis Ricci of Auburn. He is the brother of James Ricci of Santa Maria, Bob Ricci of Citrus Heights and Michael Ricci of San Jose. Grandparents are the late Elizabeth Ricci, the late Luciano Ricci, the late Corethia Davis and the late Harry Davis.
Maid of honor was Jaime Pearl of New York. Bridesmaids were London Cammack and Beth Decker of Concord, Julie Rutherford of San Mateo and Nancy Sewell, Rebecca Kenney, and Ann Gray of Santa Maria.
Best men were James Ricci and Gary Sewell. Ushers were Bob Ricci, Michael Ricci, Justin Ricci of Citrus Heights, Mitchel Sauberman and Jim Schillenger of Santa Maria.
After a honeymoon to Aruba the newlyweds are living in Belmont.
