Salem News 4-17-1924
DONALDSON- CALHOUN
At the residence of Rev. T.S. Pool in Mt. Pleasant, Iowa at 3:00 P.M. Thursday, April 10, occurred the marriage of Gerald Donaldson of Hillsboro with Miss Bonnie Calhoun of Salem. They were accompanied by the father of the bride and Hazel Donaldson a sister of the groom. The last two named subscribed their names as witnesses. The same minister united in marriage Mr. and Mrs. Samuel Donaldson, parents of the groom, 33 years ago at Salem.
