Esther H. Velott
Esther H. Velott, 89, of 2465 Edercrest Road died Saturday Dec. 26, 1998, at home after an extended illness.
Her husband, Arthur Larue "Frenchy" died in 1979.
She was a volunteer for the Williamsport Hospital, Easter Seals and the Americaqn Heart Association.
She served on the regional board of directors of the former Commonwealth Bank and Trust Co.
She was a member of the American Business Women's Association.
She was a member of the First United methodist Church, Williamsport. She had been a Sunday school teacher there.
Surviving are a son, Michael; two daughters, Suzanne V. Stroble of Loyalsock Township and Marcia H. Cromer of Aurora, Ill.; nine grandchildren; and four great grandchildren.
The funeral will be at 2 p.m. Monday, at Spitler's, 733 E. Broad St., Montoursville, with the Rev. Dr. Dennis l. Otto, her pastor officiating. Burial will be in the Montoursville Cemetery.
Friends may call the funeral home from 1 to 2 p.m. Monday.
