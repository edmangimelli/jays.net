Notes:
Salem News February 5,1920
DEATHS OF THE WEEK
COX
Waunita, infant daughter of Walter and Ada Cox, born January 27, 1920, died January 30, 1920.
A brief sojourn, but whose mission unsullied by earthly contact, who can measure?
The little flower of Paradise slipped away unaccountably, without warning, it passed to immortal arms above, without brushing the skirts of woe.
Many sympathizing friends attended the funeral at the home Sunday at 1:30. All was in keeping with the occasion. Scripture by Miss Baker, Duets by Mary Smith and Helen McMaster, and remarks by T. R. Gray.
Burial in Salem Friends cemetery.
