Throw a kick ass bike (the Schwinn S-20) into the back of the world's coolest sport truck (Dodge Dakota), and you've got yourself a Colorado downhill paradise. Ya, baby!

Of course I've never been to Colorado for downhill, but whos counting? Oh, and my Dakota is an SLT package, so there's more chrome on the front. And there's a bed liner and Hollywood bar (bike rack) mounted in the back of mine. And my truck has never been that clean...

I know nothing about the Omaha Pedalers Bicycle Club, but probably should. I live 200 yards from the Keystone Trail, and 1/4 mile from the junction of the Keystone and the Big Papio trails. There are quite a few bike trails in Omaha. There's plans to run the Cowboy Trail this summer. The Wabash Trace Trail goes from Council Bluffs into Missouri somewhere -- double track converted trail with single track technical on either side. Watch out for hosses. They're big.
