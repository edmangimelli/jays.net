 Brad and staff cornered rival Mike Morain in the Commons to extend an invitation to join the family. Accidents will happen and it just seemed fair to offer Mike a choice between the honor of Vice Presidency or sleeping with the fishes. Mike bravely but foolishly spurned The Rose and a challenge was issued for a debate. A crowd gathered and, as usual, everyone had some good laughs at Brad's expense.
